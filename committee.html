Our College Committees

At Intelvision College, we believe in holistic development through leadership, creativity, and social responsibility. Our committees provide students with opportunities to engage in various extracurricular activities and community initiatives.

Sports Committee

Headed by Prof. Anil Kumar, this committee encourages sportsmanship, organizes inter-college tournaments, and promotes a fit lifestyle among students.

NSS (National Service Scheme)

Led by Dr. Sunita Sharma, the NSS Committee conducts blood donation drives, tree plantations, and social welfare programs to instill a sense of community service in students.

Cultural Committee

Under the guidance of Prof. Rajesh Verma, this committee organizes annual fests, traditional day events, and talent showcases to celebrate India's rich culture.

Chaired by Dr. Priya Nair, the Student Council acts as the bridge between students and administration, addressing academic and non-academic concerns.
