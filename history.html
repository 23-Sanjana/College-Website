History

Founded in 1985, Intelvision College has grown from a humble community institution into a premier educational center known for academic excellence and innovation. Over the past decades, we have nurtured countless minds, equipping them with the skills and knowledge to excel in diverse fields. Our rich history is marked by a commitment to embracing new teaching methodologies, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a student-centric approach. Join us as we continue our legacy of transforming lives and fostering a brighter future.

Throughout its storied history, Intelvision College has remained steadfast in its mission to provide an inclusive and diverse learning environment. As pioneers in integrating technology into education, we have continually adapted to the ever-evolving educational landscape, ensuring our students are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern world. Our alumni, who have gone on to achieve remarkable success in various spheres, stand testament to our enduring commitment to excellence and innovation.
